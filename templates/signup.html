Wachemo Secondary and Preparatory School - Terms of Use
1. Acceptance of Terms

By creating an account on the Wachemo School portal, you agree to comply with all school policies and Ethiopian educational regulations. This platform is intended for legitimate educational purposes only.

2. User Responsibilities

All students, parents, and staff members must:

  • Provide accurate and current information during registration
  • Maintain confidentiality of login credentials
  • Use appropriate language in all communications
  • Respect intellectual property rights of all shared materials

3. Privacy Policy

Wachemo School collects necessary personal data in compliance with Ethiopia's Data Protection Proclamation. Information will be used solely for:

  • Academic record keeping
  • School communication
  • Educational service provision

4. Prohibited Activities

Users may not:

  • Share inappropriate content
  • Attempt to access unauthorized areas of the system
  • Use the platform for commercial purposes
  • Disrupt other users' educational experience

5. Academic Integrity

All submitted work must adhere to Wachemo School's honor code. Plagiarism or cheating will result in disciplinary action according to school policies.

6. Account Termination

The school administration reserves the right to suspend accounts for policy violations. Graduating students will have limited access to records post-graduation.
